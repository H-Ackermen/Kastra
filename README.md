The platform combines a **vibrant aesthetic inspired by Demon Slayer** with intuitive boards and collections, giving users a seamless experience to curate and explore ideas. Think of it as your own **Infinity Castle of ideas**, where inspiration is endless.

---

## üåü Problem Statement

Users often browse multiple platforms to find content they like, but have no unified space to **save, organize, and revisit it later**. The goal is to create a platform where:

- Users can save content from all domains.
- Content can be organized into personal collections or boards.
- Collections can be explored and filtered by category.
- Search functionality allows quick discovery of content.
- A visually engaging interface enhances the experience.
